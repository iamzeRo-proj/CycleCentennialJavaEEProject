Welcome to Cycle Centennial
Cycle Centennial is a bicycle rental service for Centennial College staff, students and guest. This service allows all registered members to rent a bicycle from any of the 3 Centennial College Scarborough campuses(Ashtonbee, Progress and Morningside).
Membership can be purchased in the following types:
  • hourly
  • daily
  • per semester
  • year

To obtain a bike:
  1. SignIn or Register
  2. Reserve an available bike
  3. Pickup your bike
  4. Return bike at any campus bike dock
